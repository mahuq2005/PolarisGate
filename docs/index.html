What PolarisGate Detects

Six safety layers running on your infrastructure — no data ever leaves your network.

🔒

Toxicity Detection

Multi-tier detection: keyword matching → BERT classifier → LLM verification. Blocks hate speech, threats, harassment, and profanity.

🛡️

PII Redaction

Detects and automatically masks SIN, SSN, email, phone, credit card, IP, passport, and more. Email→j***@***.com

🚫

Prompt Injection Protection

45 detection patterns — DAN, jailbreak attempts, system prompt leaks, role-switching, bypass attempts.

🧠

Hallucination Detection

Dual-model NLI ensemble identifies factual inaccuracies in LLM outputs. Unique in open-source guardrail tools.

📊

Dashboard & Audit Trail

Filterable incident list, policy editor with toggle switches, content testing with redacted output preview. Complete audit trail.

🌍

Multi-Language Support

Dashboard available in English, French, and Arabic. Python SDK with streaming support. Works with any LLM.
